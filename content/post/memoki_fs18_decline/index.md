A new factsheet from the [Meinungsmonitor Künstliche Intelligenz (MeMo:KI)](https://www.sozwiss.hhu.de/meinungsmonitor-kuenstliche-intelligenz) project examines a counterintuitive finding from our ongoing monitoring of AI in the German working population: rather than rising continuously, the professional use of AI has declined across successive survey waves.

The factsheet looks at the reasons behind this development, drawing on representative survey data from working people in Germany. It considers how usage patterns differ across groups and what the decline suggests for expectations about AI adoption in the workplace.

The factsheet (No. 18) is available in German via [SocArXiv](https://doi.org/10.31235/osf.io/8nyzj_v1). A translation into English will follow soon. For further results and the project dashboard, see the [MeMo:KI project page](https://www.sozwiss.hhu.de/meinungsmonitor-kuenstliche-intelligenz).
