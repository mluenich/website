Starting in April 2025, I am taking on the role of co-project lead for the [Meinungsmonitor Künstliche Intelligenz (MeMo:KI)](https://www.sozwiss.hhu.de/meinungsmonitor-kuenstliche-intelligenz) at Heinrich Heine University Düsseldorf.

MeMo:KI is a long-term research project that monitors public opinion on artificial intelligence in Germany. Through representative population surveys, the project tracks how people perceive, evaluate, and respond to AI across a range of societal domains. The project is currently funded by the German Federal Ministry of Labour and Social Affairs (BMAS).

I am looking forward to contributing to this important project and to advancing our understanding of how the German public engages with AI as a societal issue.

Results, factsheets, and the project dashboard are available on the [MeMo:KI project page](https://www.sozwiss.hhu.de/meinungsmonitor-kuenstliche-intelligenz).
